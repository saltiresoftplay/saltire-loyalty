Common questions

Things parents ask before booking.

Can I bring my own birthday cake?
Yes, absolutely — most parents do. We'll keep it safely until cake-and-candles time, and we've got plates and napkins ready.
What ages are the parties suitable for?
Both packages work brilliantly for kids from about 1 to 10. The frame has a separate under-3 area, so younger guests aren't left out. For exclusive hire we can adapt the day around any age range.
Are adults charged on a party?
Adults are always free at Saltire — including on parties. So bring as many parents and grandparents as you'd like.
What does "soft drinks" actually mean?
For party packages, drinks means diluting juice (Robinsons) and water — unlimited refills. We've found that's what the kids actually want and parents prefer over fizzy options.
What if a child can't eat the buffet?
Tell us when you book and we'll work around it — allergies, dietary needs, picky eaters. We'd rather know in advance than scramble on the day.
How far in advance should I book?
Weekend slots in school terms book up fastest — aim for 4-6 weeks ahead if you can. Mid-week is usually more flexible. Exclusive hire generally needs more notice — get in touch as soon as you've got a date in mind.
What happens if we need to cancel?
We'll always try to move your booking to another date. Specifics on the deposit are covered in our terms and conditions, which we'll share with you when you book.
Can we have decorations?
You're very welcome to bring your own. The party room is yours for the 45 minutes — string up the bunting, set out the bubbles, do your thing.
