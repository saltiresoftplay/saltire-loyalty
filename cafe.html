Inside the café

Allergens & dietary needs

Just have a word with the team.

We'd rather chat at the counter than guess from a label. Tell us what's needed — we'll work around allergies, intolerances, or fussy eaters as best we can.

Allergies

Always ask before ordering — our team can talk you through what's in each item and any cross-contact risks.

Vegetarian options

Always something on the board — pasta, sandwiches, soup, sweet treats. Just ask if anything's not clear.

Picky eaters

We get it. The kids' menu is built around what kids actually eat, and we can swap most things to keep them happy.
