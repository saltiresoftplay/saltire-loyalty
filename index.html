The Soft Play

Plenty of space to run riot, plenty of space to sit down.

The frame is big enough that it's a proper adventure for the older kids, and small enough that you can keep an eye on yours without losing them. The under-3 zone is separate, so the wee ones get a wee world of their own.

Birthdays & Parties

Two ways to throw the perfect birthday.

Whether you want the buzz of the room full of regulars or the place entirely to yourselves, we've got you. Both packages include a proper buffet (you choose hot or cold) and unlimited diluting juice for the kids.

Most popular

Regular Soft Play Party

£17 per child · 2 hours

The classic. 75 minutes of soft play and 45 minutes in the party room — plenty of time to play, eat, and open presents without feeling rushed.

  • Min 10, max 25 kids
  • Buffet (hot or cold) included
  • Unlimited diluting juice & water
  • £100 deposit secures the date

Have the place to yourselves

Exclusive Hire Party

£17 per child · min 20 kids · no max

The whole soft play is closed to the public. Just your guests, the frame, the party room and the café. Perfect for big groups, sensory needs, or anyone who'd rather avoid strangers on the day.

  • 100% exclusive use, 2 hours
  • £150 deposit secures the venue closure
  • Hot food: pizza, nuggets, chips
  • Unlimited diluting juice & water
