Common questions

Things groups ask before booking.

Why is the deposit higher than the regular party?
Because we're closing the venue to the public for your slot, the £150 deposit covers what we'd otherwise be earning from walk-in customers. It's deducted from your final bill on the day, not on top.
What if we have fewer than 20 kids on the day?
The minimum spend is calculated as 20 × £17 = £340. If your final headcount drops below 20, you'll still be billed for the minimum so the venue closure makes sense for everyone. Tell us if numbers shift dramatically — we can sometimes work with you.
Is there really no upper limit?
Practically, fire and capacity rules apply — but the venue comfortably handles big groups. Tell us your numbers and we'll confirm fit on the call.
Can we have a cold buffet for exclusive parties?
Exclusive hire is hot food — pizza, chicken nuggets, and chips. If you've got specific dietary needs that don't fit that menu, tell us when you book and we'll work around it.
Are adults charged?
No. Adults are always free at Saltire, including on exclusive parties. Bring as many parents and grandparents as you like.
How far in advance do we need to book?
Exclusive hire generally needs more notice than a regular party — at least 6 weeks for weekend slots in school terms, sometimes more. Get in touch as soon as you've got a date in mind.
Can we bring our own decorations and cake?
Absolutely — and with exclusive hire you've got the run of the place to set it up however you want. Just tell us in advance if there's anything specific we need to plan around.
Is there a regular (non-exclusive) party option?
Yes — same £17 per child rate, minimum 10 kids, £100 deposit, and the soft play stays open to the public. See the regular party page.
