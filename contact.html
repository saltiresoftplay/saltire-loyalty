How to find us

Mayfield Industrial Estate, just south of Edinburgh.

Easy from the A7, Edinburgh, the Lothians and Borders. Free, on-site parking — no meters, no faff. We're in Unit 1, the very front of the estate.

By car
From Edinburgh: A7 south, follow signs for Mayfield. From the Borders: A7 north, signed Mayfield Industrial Estate.
By bus
Lothian Buses route 29 stops nearby. Walk approx 5–7 minutes from the closest stop.
Parking
Free, plentiful, on-site. Buggy access through the main door.
Accessibility
Step-free entrance, accessible toilet on-site. Just give us a ring beforehand if there's anything specific we can prepare for.
