The Soft Play

A proper adventure for the older kids, a wee world for the toddlers.

No need to book. Walk in, pay at reception, stay as long as you like. The frame's big enough for the big kids, and there's a separate corner kept just for the under-3s.

Two play zones, one room

Different ages, different worlds.

We've split the soft play in two — a big-kid frame for the running, climbing, sliding, hiding crowd, and a calmer toddler corner so the wee ones get a wee world of their own.

Before you visit

A few things worth knowing.

Nothing complicated. The kind of stuff a friend would mention before sending you our way.

Socks on, please

Socks must be worn on the frame at all times — by kids and adults if you're going up with them. We sell spares at reception if anyone forgets.

Adults supervise

Children remain the responsibility of their accompanying adult. We're here to look after the place; you look after the wee ones.

No outside food

Our café is right there with hot & cold options, fresh coffee, and freshly-baked scones. You won't go hungry.

Buggy parking

Plenty of room to park buggies inside. We'll keep an eye while you keep an eye on the slide.

Allergies welcome

Just speak to the team at the café counter — they'll help you find something that works.
