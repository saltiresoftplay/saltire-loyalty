Our Story

One branch. One team. One focus.

Saltire Soft Play is independent and family-run — one place, properly looked after, in Mayfield, Newtongrange. We built the kind of soft play we wished existed when our own kids were small.

[Photo: the team / family running Saltire]

Why we exist

A wee place we'd want to bring our own kids to.

Saltire Soft Play started with a simple thought — there had to be a better version of this. Somewhere clean, somewhere warm, somewhere parents could actually sit and have a proper coffee while the kids burned themselves out. So we built it.

We're not a chain, we're not a franchise, and we don't have a head office in another city telling us what to do. We're independent. One small business in Mayfield, looked after by people who care whether the floor's clean and whether the coffee's any good.

Most of our customers come back. Many become regulars — the loyalty card sees plenty of action. We treat the regulars like neighbours because, mostly, they are.

What we care about

Three things we won't compromise on.

There's a lot we could put on a list. These are the three that matter.

01

Properly clean

The frame gets cleaned every day. The toilets get checked through the day. The café tables get wiped between every group. It's not glamorous — it's just non-negotiable.

02

Honest pricing

No booking fees. No surge pricing. No "premium" tier with a fancier bouncy castle. The price you see at reception is the price.

03

Properly local

We employ local. We buy local where we can. We're part of the same community as the families who walk through the door — and we like it that way.
